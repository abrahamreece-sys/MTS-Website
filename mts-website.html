Megan Holder has worked in live production since 1995 — over three decades spanning theatre, touring shows, and major international events, including a stint as Technical Delivery & Crew Manager for Expo 2020 in Dubai. A multiple-award-winning sound designer for orchestral and musical productions, she now runs Megan's Technical Services with one principle: deliver exactly what a client needs, build it to last, and never sell anyone equipment they don't need.
